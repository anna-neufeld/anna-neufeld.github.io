Mentoring

Statistics and Probability Association Directed Reading Program (SPA-DRP)

  • Co-founded the SPA-DRP with Emily Flanagan at the University of Washington. The program, modeled after Directed Reading Programs in mathematics at several other universities, pairs undergraduate students with phd student mentors for quarter long independent reading projects.
  • Serving on graduate student organizing committee for 2020-2022. Duties include graduate/undergraduate student recruitment, undergraduate student admissions, event planning, and website maintaining.
  • Mentored 8 undergraduate students in topics including natural language processing, disease modeling, multiple testing, and computational biology from Winter 2020 through Spring 2022.

About

Hi! I am an assistant professor of statistics at Williams College. From 2023-2024, I was a postdoctoral researcher at the Fred Hutchinson Cancer Center in Seattle, advised by Jeff Leek . In 2023, I received my PhD from the Department of Statistics at the University of Washington, where I was advised by Daniela Witten. I am interested in problems related to "double dipping" and selective inference, and I am particularly motivated by applications to genomic data. Before UW, I received undergraduate degrees in mathematics and computer science at Williams College. I am passionate about statistics education and love teaching and working with undergraduates.
